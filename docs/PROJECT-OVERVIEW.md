# Project Overview — Attack-Surface & IAM Governance Intelligence

## Lighthouse Technology | Domain 4 | Task 3

This project demonstrates how identity and access management data can be transformed into governance-grade risk intelligence.

Rather than treating IAM as a periodic access-review exercise, the workflow models identity relationships, privileged access, administrative paths, service-account exposure, and high-value asset reachability. It then converts those findings into residual-risk scores, blast-radius analytics, framework-mapped remediation actions, validation requirements, and evidence-backed executive reporting.

## Business Problem

Traditional IAM reviews often answer:

- Who has access?
- Is an account active?
- Is a group membership approved?

This project extends the question:

> What can an identity reach, what could be abused, how far could impact spread, and which remediation action reduces the most risk?
